Why Audiobooks?

For me, the best thing about Audibooks is the convenience. I love reading a good book, but it's hard to carve out the time to do so. With audibooks, I don't have to choose between doing chores or reading a book; I can do both! Plus, it makes those chores a lot more enjoyable. I also love listening to audibooks when I run, when I'm doing (limited) auto-pilot tasks at work, and of course, on long road trips. I hate being in the car for long periods of time, but I've been in situations where I can't wait to get on the road again just so I can keep listening to the story. The simple fact that they make otherwise mundane or unpleasant tasks enjoyable seals the deal for me. Maybe they'll do the same for you!

Start Listening

My favorite way to listen to audibooks is through Audible, where you pay a monthly fee for 1 or 2 books a month (among other member benefits). If you'd prefer not to pay for them, it's likely your local library has them available to rent. There's also the website LibriVox, where you can find free audibooks for books in the public domain.
